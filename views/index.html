Introduction
Whether you just moved to a new area or are just visiting, it is hard to get familiar with new surroundings. In a highly globalized world, access to travel and long distance relocation has become more widely available, so that people can visit more new places. However, the travel experience can be tainted by unfamiliarity to the surrounding areas. Being lost and stressing out is no way to travel. As lovers of travelling, our group wanted to find a solution for newcomers and visitors to new areas to feeling welcomed and accustomed to the place.
About Our App
We created an iOS app, called ArrivAR, that allows users to interact with other users, by posting status updates that will pop up on the screen. The post will be anchored in midair, near the location, where the status was posted, so that different users can interact with each other with a greater sense of spatial closeness. ArrivAR also acts as a tour guide for the user, as historical and interesting facts about a certain location will be posted in mid-air similar to the status updates. These facts will help the traveler or the newcomer to get to know the new place. Lastly, ArrivAR acts an advertisement site for nearby deals and job posting site for nearby jobs. Users can find good deals learn about good places to shop and eat nearby. Also, users who are interested in jobs will be able to see job posting in the air and click on the ad to get information about the job. This will especially help those who moved into a new area to find a job.
